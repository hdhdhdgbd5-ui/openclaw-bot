1. **Reddit**: Find subreddit with problem your product solves
   - Example: r/Notion for Notion templates

2. **Twitter/X**: Thread explaining problem + solution
   - Last tweet links to product

3. **Product Hunt**: Launch your tool (free exposure)

4. **YouTube**: Review video or tutorial (content empire crossover)
