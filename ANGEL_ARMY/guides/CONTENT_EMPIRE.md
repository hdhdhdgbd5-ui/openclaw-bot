## How It Works

### Step 1: Angel Finds Viral Topic
Angel searches Reddit/YouTube/TikTok for:
- Questions people keep asking
- Topics with high engagement
- Low competition keywords

### Step 2: Angel Creates Content
Types of content Angel can make:
1. **YouTube Shorts scripts** (60 sec)
2. **TikTok voiceovers**
3. **Reddit posts that get upvotes**
4. **Blog articles**

### Step 3: You Review
- Read the script/post
- Watch the video idea
- Click YES (publish) or NO (skip)

### Step 4: If YES - You Create
Option A: Simple (Recommended)
- Use CapCut templates
- Add AI voiceover or text-to-speech
- Post to YouTube Shorts + TikTok

Option B: Advanced
- Record yourself (better engagement)
- Angel edits and adds captions
