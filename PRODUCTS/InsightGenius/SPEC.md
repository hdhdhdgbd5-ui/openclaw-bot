# InsightGenius - AI Data Analytics & Visualization

## 📊 What Is This?

InsightGenius transforms raw data into beautiful visualizations and AI-powered insights. Paste your CSV data, get instant charts, trend analysis, and actionable recommendations.

## 💼 Perfect For:

- Business analysts
- Marketing managers
- Small business owners
- Anyone working with data

## 📱 Features

✅ **Visual Charts**: Bar and line chart generation
✅ **AI Insights**: Automated pattern detection
✅ **Trend Analysis**: Spot growth and decline
✅ **Anomaly Detection**: Find unusual patterns
✅ **CSV Import**: Easy data upload
✅ **Export Options**: PNG, PDF, CSV

## 🚀 How To Deploy

### Option 1: GitHub Pages
1. Upload to GitHub repository
2. Enable Pages in settings

### Option 2: Netlify
1. Drag & drop folder
2. Get instant URL
