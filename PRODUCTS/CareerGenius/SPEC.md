# CareerGenius - AI Resume Builder & Career Coach

## 💼 What Is This?

CareerGenius creates professional, ATS-friendly resumes in minutes. Enter your experience and skills, get a polished resume with quantified achievements and expert formatting.

## 💼 Perfect For:

- Job seekers
- Career changers
- Professionals seeking promotions
- Graduates entering workforce

## 📱 Features

✅ **ATS-Friendly Format**: Passes all applicant tracking systems
✅ **Auto Achievement Quantification**: Numbers make impact
✅ **Cover Letter Generator**: Matching templates
✅ **LinkedIn Optimization**: Profile enhancement tips
✅ **Multiple Templates**: Various styles
✅ **Score Analysis**: Resume strength rating
