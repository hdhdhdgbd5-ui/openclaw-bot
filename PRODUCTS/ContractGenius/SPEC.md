# ContractGenius - AI Legal Contract Generator

## 📜 What Is This?

ContractGenius generates professional legal contracts in minutes using AI. Select contract type, enter party details, and get ready-to-sign documents with proper legal language.

## 💼 Perfect For:

- Freelancers needing client agreements
- Small business owners
- Entrepreneurs launching startups
- Landlords renting properties

## 📱 Features

✅ **6 Contract Types**: NDA, Freelance, Employment, Partnership, Lease, Sales
✅ **Auto-Fill Parties**: Enter names, dates, terms
✅ **PDF Export**: Download ready-to-sign documents
✅ **Professional Templates**: Industry-standard legal language
✅ **Signature Lines**: Ready for signing
✅ **Legal Notices**: Disclaimer included
